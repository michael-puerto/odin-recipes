Description

small description of the cookie recipie I wanted

ingredients

  • butter
  • sugar
  • egg
  • peanut butter

steps

  1. set oven to 300 degrees
  2. line a tray with allimunim foil and oil up
  3. in a bowl mix the butter and sugar together
  4. once mixed add the egg and peanut butter and mix again
  5. use an ice cream scoop and put on tray
  6. leave it in the oven for 30 minutes or until golden brown
