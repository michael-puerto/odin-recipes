Description

small description of the lasgna recipie that is my grandmas favorite.

ingredients

  • tomato sauce
  • pasta
  • ricotta cheese

steps

  1. set oven to 300 degrees
  2. start by layering a sheet of pasta
  3. add a layer of tomato sauce
  4. then follow with a layer of ricotta cheese
  5. repeat until tray is full
  6. set in the oven for 40 minutes and serve!
